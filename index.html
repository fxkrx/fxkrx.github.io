Hi, I’m Fikri Harun! I’m Fikri Harun! a creative professional transitioning from the culinary arts to the world of graphic design. My journey began as a pastry chef, where I spent over five years perfecting the art of presentation and storytelling through food. This passion for aesthetics eventually led me to explore visual arts beyond the plate, sparking my interest in design and photography. Along the way, I developed skills in photography and photo/video editing, capturing moments and enhancing visuals to tell compelling stories. Currently, I’m expanding my expertise by pursuing a diploma in graphic design, where I’m learning the fundamentals of branding, layout design, and digital media. I’m a firm believer in lifelong learning, and I approach every project with curiosity and enthusiasm. While I’m still growing as a designer, my unique background and ability to adapt creatively give me a fresh perspective. I’m eager to collaborate, learn, and create impactful designs that connect with people. When I’m not studying or working on projects, you’ll find me with a camera in hand, exploring inspiration through photography or experimenting with new dessert recipes. Let’s create something extraordinary together!
